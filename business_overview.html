RedFoot Custom Prints Business Overview

BUSINESS OVERVIEW

OUR VISION

RedFoot Custom Prints will be the top choice company in the Phoenix area for small to medium sized businesses to order and receive comfortable, quality branded t-shirts while at the same time helping preserve Arizona's natural beauty through conservation donations.

OUR MISSION

Using continuing graphic design and production innovations, RedFoot Custom Prints collaborates with customers from the design stage to delivery so they have the branded t-shirts they desire that inspire their own brand loyalty at highly competitive prices.

THE PRODUCT WE PROVIDE

RedFoot Custom Prints provides design consultation and various custom apparel printing packages to serve small to medium sized businesses.

MARKET ANALYSIS

WHO WE ARE TARGETING

RedFoot Custom Print's target audience is small to medium sized metro Phoenix area businesses such as restaurants, sports clubs, repair companies, etc., who wish to grow their brand identification and marketing with innovative apparel designs.

THE PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING

We wish to be the first choice solution for customers who would like to work with a company that provides ongoing customer support from initial consultation to delivery. This way, the customer doesn't feel that they're alone in the process of developing branded t-shirts. Also, RedFoot Custom Prints is dedicated to using a portion of all profits to benefit Arizona land and wildlife conservation efforts.

OUR COMPETITORS

Our competitors in the t-shirt design and production space come from other local printers, online services such as CustomInk, promotional supply businesses and online freelancers.

OUR COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

RedFoot Custom Prints works to be ready and available to help its customers every step in the t-shirt design and production process so a customer doesn't have to worry that they're not getting what they expect in branded t-shirts. The communication process includes traditional ordering techniques such as telephone to more technologically advanced options such as an automated online ordering system.

MARKETING AND SALES PLAN

MARKETING CHANNELS

RedFoot Custom Prints will make active use of its website to be a quick customer resource and potential sales funnel. The use of various social media platforms will also be used for content marketing and paid ads. Email marketing will also be utilized as more businesses or individuals reach out and give permission to contact them.

MARKETING MATERIALS

We will use business cards and flyers that can be distributed to potential customers. Also, website content and AI generated images will be kept for marketing purposes. Video marketing content will be created as well and distributed online through our website or social media platforms.

PRICING STRATEGY

Pricing will have to be highly competitive for the majority of t-shirt package offerings. This will require low overhead and utilization of online networking and word-of-mouth strategies.

DISTRIBUTION CHANNELS

For very local clients in the Phoenix area, pick up and personal delivery options will be available. If a customer is outside of the 20 mile range, USPS or UPS delivery will be utilized.

KEY OBJECTIVES AND SUCCESS METRICS

OBJECTIVES WE PLAN TO ACHIEVE IN A GIVEN TIME FRAME (SIX MONTHS) AND HOW WE'LL MEASURE THEM

Customer Acquisition

Within the first six months, we'll need to acquire at least 20 new customers, some of whom will be recurring.

Sales Objectives

Within the first six months, revenue for 20 new customers can range from $100 to $2000 per order. Sales objective range can be from $2000 to $40000 in these parameters for six months total. The sales goal will be $8000 revenue per month for the first six months.

Marketing Goals

For the first six months, marketing will consist of website development that allows customers to learn about package offerings, AZ conservation efforts and various options to reach us. Search Engine Optimization will be applied to the website so it appears on Google search for local t-shirt printers. A minimum of 3 posts per week will be done for Facebook pages, Instagram account and Pinterest account for RedFoot Custom Prints as well as its CrypticCreatures.shop brand.
